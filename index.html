Rainbow Digital AI

AI that helps small businesses and individuals organize documents and receipts — simply and securely.

Our mission is to make life easier by turning messy files, documents, and receipts into clear, organized insights — without installing AI models, managing cloud services, or writing any code. Just upload and let Rainbow Digital do the work.

AI Search + Chat

Upload documents and instantly chat with your data using deep semantic search and AI-powered understanding. Perfect for real-estate agents, consultants, and professionals who need fast insights from large document sets.

Example:
Q: Does this HOA cover earthquake insurance?
